Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At GIGA TRAVELCATIONS, we prioritize the privacy and security of our clients' personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, protect, and use the information you provide when using our websites, services, and smart travel booking tools.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide, manage, and optimize our luxury travel packages and aviation services. This includes:

  • ✔
    Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, telephone number, mailing address, and booking preferences.
  • ✔
    Travel Details: Booking history, destination choices, passport information (when required for charters), and customized travel requirements.
  • ✔
    Usage & Device Data: IP addresses, browser types, cookies, and page interaction details gathered dynamically through your visits.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to facilitate smart, safe, and luxurious travel experiences:

  • ✔
    To process and confirm your custom travel bookings, packages, and charter requests.
  • ✔
    To communicate essential travel updates, safety briefings, and customized flight itineraries.
  • ✔
    To enhance our website interface, responsive tools, and personalize future user interactions.

3. Data Security and Sharing

We deploy strict physical, administrative, and technical guardrails to protect your personal details against unauthorized access. We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with trusted operators, hospitality partners, and regulatory authorities when strictly necessary to execute your travel and flight plans.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:
